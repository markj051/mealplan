Away Days Tips

🧳 What to Bring from Home

  • Pre-made white bean soup in containers (optional)
  • Hard-boiled eggs from your flock (Thu breakfast)
  • Fresh avocados, lemons, herbs
  • Chicken thighs & shrimp in a cooler if driving
  • Overnight oat jar already prepped for Thu night

🛒 Pick Up on Arrival

  • Fresh zucchini, cherry tomatoes, cucumber
  • Arugula or spinach (small bag)
  • Sweet potato (1–2)
  • Whole grain bread & pita
  • Shrimp (frozen is fine)
  • Fruit for snacking

⚡ Keeping It Simple

  • Three nights, two of which are leftover-based lunches
  • Slow cooker soup = set it and forget it for Wed
  • Shrimp stir-fry is under 20 minutes start to finish
  • Overnight oats take 2 minutes to prep the night before
  • Lentil bowl components travel well — prep Wed evening

❤️ Staying on Track Away

  • All meals follow the same heart-healthy Mediterranean approach
  • Sodium stays low — stick to herbs, lemon & garlic for flavor
  • Keep almonds & walnuts on hand for easy snacking
  • Avoid the temptation to grab takeout — these meals are fast
  • Hydrate well especially on workout days
