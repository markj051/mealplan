Notes & Guidelines

❤️ Your Health Goals

  • Keep sodium low — use herbs, lemon & garlic instead of salt
  • Olive oil replaces butter throughout
  • Fatty fish (salmon, cod) 2–3×/week supports cholesterol
  • Beans & lentils provide fiber that lowers LDL
  • Walnuts & almonds are your best snack — heart-healthy fats
  • Stay well hydrated on workout days

👧 Her Dairy-Free Needs

  • All home meals are naturally dairy-free or easily adapted
  • Almond milk for oats & smoothies
  • Tahini provides calcium without dairy
  • Avocado or hummus for richness instead of cheese
  • Her active lifestyle needs plenty of calories — don't restrict

🐔 Backyard Eggs

  • Use your own eggs for Tuesday scrambled breakfast
  • Hard-boil 3–4 extra Sunday for grab-and-go snacks
  • Backyard eggs are higher in omega-3s than store-bought
  • Skip the egg aisle entirely — you're covered!

🌾 Oats Note

  • Only rolled (old-fashioned) oats work for overnight oats
  • Steel-cut oats won't soften from soaking — cook stovetop if preferred
  • Quick oats work in a pinch but turn mushier
